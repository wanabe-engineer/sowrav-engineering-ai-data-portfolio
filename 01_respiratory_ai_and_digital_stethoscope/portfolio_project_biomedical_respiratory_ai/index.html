Project Narrative

What this demonstrates

Research Problem

Can respiratory sound recordings from accessible hardware support screening workflows for pediatric pneumonia or abnormal lung sounds?

Technical Pipeline

Acquisition, denoising, segmentation, spectral features, model training, validation, and clinical limitation analysis.
